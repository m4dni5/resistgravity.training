# 06 — Exercise Identity, Motivation, and Psychology of RT

**Key Finding:** Self-Determination Theory (autonomy, competence, relatedness) is the strongest psychological framework for predicting exercise adherence. Developing an exercise identity ("I am a lifter") is associated with long-term maintenance. Intrinsic motivation outperforms extrinsic goals (aesthetics) for sustained behavior.
