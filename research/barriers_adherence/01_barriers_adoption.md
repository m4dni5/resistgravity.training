# 01 — Barriers to Resistance Training Adoption in 40+ Adults

**Key Finding:** The most commonly reported barriers to RT adoption among middle-aged and older adults include lack of time, fear of injury, gym intimidation, insufficient knowledge of how to perform exercises, and a lack of exercise identity. These barriers are modifiable and represent clear service design opportunities.
