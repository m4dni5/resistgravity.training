# 02 — Resistance Training Adherence and Dropout

**Key Finding:** RT program dropout rates are high, with many studies showing 30-50% attrition within the first 3-6 months. Key predictors of dropout include program complexity, lack of perceived progress, time burden, boredom, and injury. Simpler programs, social support, and autonomy-supportive coaching improve adherence.

**Implications:** Complexity kills consistency. Programs that minimize cognitive load, provide clear progression signals, and keep time commitment low have better retention. For service design: default to simple, make complexity opt-in.
