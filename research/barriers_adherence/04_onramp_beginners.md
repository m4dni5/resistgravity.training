# 04 — The On-Ramp: How Beginners Should Start RT

**Key Finding:** Beginners benefit significantly from supervised instruction, starting with simple movement patterns, and following progressive overload from low volumes. Measurable strength gains appear within 4-8 weeks. The transition from beginner to intermediate requires deliberate progression in volume and complexity.

**Implications:** The first 8 weeks are critical. Supervision (even remote) dramatically improves outcomes and safety. A service for non-lifters should provide: simple starting program, form guidance, clear progression milestones, and a path from "never lifted" to "independent lifter."
