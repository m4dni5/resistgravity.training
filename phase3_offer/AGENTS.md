# Phase 3: The Offer Synthesis

## Context

Resist Gravity is a personal training business by Matt in Truckee, CA. We have completed three rounds of research:

- **Benefits research** (research/benefits/): 15 categories, 90+ papers on why RT matters
- **Phase 1** (research/effective_rt/): 30 papers on what makes RT effective for health
- **Phase 2** (research/barriers_adherence/): 67 papers on why people don't start or don't progress

Phase 3 synthesizes all of this into Matt's actual service design.

## Product Architecture (Decided)

| Product | Format | Delivery | Status |
|---------|--------|----------|--------|
| On-Ramp Program | Standalone Everfit program | Self-directed | In design |
| Follow-Up Programs (1-3) | Standalone Everfit programs | Self-directed | Pending |

| Consultation | One-time, hourly rate | In-person or remote | Existing |

## Two Audiences

**Audience A — On-Ramp (No Current RT Routine)**
- Problem: Don't know where to start, intimidated, time-constrained
- Product: On-ramp Everfit program (8 weeks)
- Path: On-ramp → follow-up program or monthly online

**Audience B — Diagnostic (RT Routine, No Results)**
- Problem: Training but not progressing, plateaued, frustrated
- Product: Full diagnostic questionnaire + consultation
- Path: Diagnostic → consultation → implementation plan (may include on-ramp or follow-up)
